About Me

After roughly ten years of professional experience, it feels like I've done a little bit of just about everything! Well, definitely not everything, but from Christian ministry to e-learning development, to Autism therapy, to energy trading, to database management - a wide variety for sure! Through it all, a few prominent trends and patterns have emerged that describe well what I bring to the table.
I am:
Analytical I absolutely love analyzing complex systems, data sets, people, and situations to try to make sense of it all and tell a "story" that might lead to insight.

A Problem-Solver To me, a problem is simply an opportunity wating to happen! I revel at the opportunity to apply detailed analysis to understanding complex problems and generating viable solutions.

Creative Yea, I write a little and play a little guitar (poorly), but that's not what I mean here. Again and again I have found that solutions to technical problems can be creative - beautiful even!

A Servant Solving a problem is extremely satisfying all its own - but all the more when someone can benefit from the solution. This is what gets me out of bed in the morning - the hope for the opportunity to use my unique set of skills, abilities, and experiences to make life better for someone.
